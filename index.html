About Green Horizon Initiative

Green Horizon Initiative (GHI) is committed to addressing climate change through sustainable environmental practices in underprivileged communities across Africa.

Our Team

John Doe - Founder & CEO

Jane Smith - Project Manager

Vision & Mission

Vision: To create a sustainable future for all through community-driven environmental stewardship.

Mission: Empowering communities to take charge of their environment and lead the way in sustainable practices.

Our Story

Founded in 2018, GHI has grown from a small grassroots movement to a leading NGO in environmental conservation, reaching thousands across Africa.

Our Partners

Awards & Recognition

GHI has received numerous awards for its impactful work, including the Green Earth Award in 2022.

Our Future Goals

GHI aims to plant 1 million trees by 2026 and expand its clean water initiatives to 50 more villages.
